HOW SKKY WORKS


WHO

At Skky, we have a team of dedicated Skky managers who take care of your Airbnb guests and manage all bookings so you don't have to lift a finger.


WHAT

Skky is commited to maximising your rental returns whether you have a house, apartment or a single bedroom to rent out. We look after:

Marketing: We create professional listings on short term rental platforms like Airbnb.

Guest Support: We promptly manage all guest inquiries from booking until check-out to ensure guests have a great stay.

Dynamic Pricing: We utilise rental pricing algorithms to ensure your listing is competitive when measured against comparable market supply and demand to maximise your occupancy rates and rental returns.

Skky Housekeepers: Our experienced Skky Housekeepers will quickly and efficiently clean your home from top to bottom for same day check-ins.

Security: We screen all guests to ensure your home is safe and treated with respect.


WHEN

Whether you own an investment property or will be travelling for a period so your home is vacant, the team at Skky can offer you specialised property management services to help you maximise your rental returns.


WHERE

We focus on properties in the Sydney CBD as well as inner city suburbs such as Pyrmont, Surry Hills, Darlinghurst and Glebe which are ideal for short term leasing due to their close proximity to the city. However we are not limited to these areas and are always happy to discuss with you whether short term rentals are the best method of maximising your rental yield.


WHY

Are you going on a holiday soon? Are you after a higher return on your investment property? Skky is a great solution that will see you making the most of successful digital platforms such as Airbnb. Our team will provide you with effortless income while you are away or tap into this rapidly growing opportunity to potentially produce greater returns than the traditional property leasing method.
