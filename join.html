HOW TO GET STARTED


When you join Skky, our team will look after you in taking the work and stress out of short term property management while at all times looking to maximise your rental returns.

If you are a new host, simply follow the easy steps below to get started:

Connect with us: Visit our contact us page and we will get in touch with you at a mutually convenient time.

Meeting you and your home: It is important that we understand your needs and expectations before you hand over the keys to us. We will meet with you to discuss the process and what is involved, before conducting a full home tour to develop your home welcome manual for guests.

That's it, we will take care of the rest: At all times you can access your account calendar to view upcoming bookings, payouts and conversations we are having with guests.

Remember that guests book their holiday accomodation weeks and at times months in advance, so contact us soon as possible so you can lock in future bookings!
