MAXIMISE THE RETURN ON YOUR PROPERTY


At Skky, we have two different pricing models for managing your property.

Long term Hosting: Sydney is listed as one of the global top 10 cities for short term holiday rentals where you can increase your rental returns when compared with traditional long term renting to a single tenant. Also, you will have the flexibility to block out dates in the calendar if ever you want to stay at the property any time during the year. We take a 15% property management fee from each booking under long term hosting. Our long term hosting option is classed as management for 3 months and greater.

Holiday Hosting: Want to utilise your vacant property to make money while you are away on holidays or for business? Our short term hosting option is a great solution that can see you earning money to cover for some of those costs while you’re away. We take a 20% property management fee from each booking under this option.

Under both the holiday hosting and long term hosting packages, payments are made to you the day after guests check-in.

The following are included in the property management fee:

  • property listing creation
  • handling guest screening and booking inquiries
  • meet guests for check-in or lockbox set-ups
  • 24/7 guest communication during their stay
  • arranging Skky housekeepers for cleaning and laundry on check-out
  • arranging minor property maintenance
  • monthly report so you can see your rental income and expenses

Other fees not included in the property management fee:

  • Professional cleaning and detailing after each booking – depending on the size of your property, cleaning fees (including laundry of new sheets) start at $70. All cleaning fees will be paid by the guest, not you.
  • Minor property maintenance – in the unfortunate event of a broken kettle or burnt out light bulb, we will take care of any minor maintenance to keep your guest happy. The cost will be reimbursed by you.
